Chipped Beef Cheese Ball

Ingredients

  • 1 – 8oz. Cream Cheese
  • 1 – 4oz. pkg. Chipped Beef chopped & split in half
  • 1 green onion – All (or several) chopped

Directions

  1. Combine cream cheese, ½ of chipped beef & onion in bowl.
  2. Mix completely & form in to a ball.
  3. Roll in remaining beef.
  4. Serve with crackers.
