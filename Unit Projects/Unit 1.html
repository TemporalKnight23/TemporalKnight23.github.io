Michael's Salsa Recipe

Yield: 3-6 quarts or pints

Ready In: 1-2 Hours

Ingredients

  • 8-10 Roma Tomatoes Chopped
  • 2 Sweet Onions Chopped
  • 2 Green Peppers Chopped
  • 1-2 Jalapeno Peppers Chopped
  • 6 Garlic Cloves Minced
  • 2 teaspoons Cumin
  • 2 teaspoon Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Cilantro
  • 1/8 cup Canning Salt
  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1/3 cup Vinegar
  • 1 (15 ounce) can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 (12 ounce) can Tomato Paste

I use a Food Processer to chop my vegetables.

Directions

  1. Mix all together.
  2. Bring to a slow boil for 10 minute.
  3. Seal in jars and cook in hot water bath for 10 minute.

Use a 16 Quart Stock Pot to boil the ingredients.

This is a medium salsa.
Leave seeds for extra spicy!
This is also a chunky salsa so if you want a smoother salsa cut your veggies into smaller pieces.
